Refund Policy

Effective date: November 21, 2025

This Refund Policy explains when refunds are available for non-tangible, one-time performance audit and technical consulting services delivered by Fastrank, operated by Cadunlock LLC (“we”, “us”, or “our”).

By purchasing an audit or consulting engagement from Fastrank, you agree to this Refund Policy in addition to our Terms of Service.

1. Our 14-Day Delivery Guarantee (“The Trust Principle”)

We guarantee that we will deliver the core audit you paid for.

If, for reasons attributable to Fastrank, we are unable to:

  • access your platform or website after you have provided the requested access details, or
  • deliver the core audit findings within 14 calendar days of the purchase date,

you are entitled to a full refund of the audit fee paid for that order.

2. Refund Eligibility & Right of Withdrawal (US / EU Compliance)

Our work generally starts shortly after you provide access and complete the intake questionnaire. Because the service involves manual analysis, expertise, and intellectual property, different rules apply before and after delivery.

A. US & Global Standard – Non-Refundable After Delivery

Once we have completed and delivered the main deliverables, the service is considered fully rendered and is non-refundable.

The service is considered delivered when you receive:

  • the final technical audit report (PDF or web document), and
  • a custom implementation roadmap / action plan.

Our commitment is to provide a clear, actionable plan designed to help your site target 90+ Core Web Vitals and PageSpeed scores wherever realistically achievable.

Because the final live performance depends on how (and whether) you or your developer implement the recommendations, we do not issue refunds based on the final PageSpeed score or unfinished implementation.

B. EU Right of Withdrawal – Digital Services Clause

For customers located in the European Union, the standard 14-day “Right of Withdrawal” for distance contracts does not apply once a digital service has begun with your prior consent.

By purchasing an audit, providing access details, and confirming your order, you:

  • request the immediate start of the digital service, and
  • acknowledge that you waive your 14-day right of withdrawal once we begin the technical analysis and audit processing.

This is a standard requirement for bespoke digital and consulting services that are delivered quickly and cannot be “returned” once the work has started.

3. Compliance & Transparency (Stripe & Google Ads)

This Refund Policy is designed to protect both the client and Fastrank and to remain compliant with major payment and advertising platforms.

  • Service nature: Our audits and recommendations are non-tangible, time-based consulting and intellectual-property services.
  • Stripe compliance: Once the audit report and roadmap have been delivered, the service is considered “received as described”, which limits disputes based on “Service Not Received”.
  • Google Ads / SEO trust: Being explicit about how we deliver, what we guarantee, and when refunds apply supports transparency and improves the trust signals around our brand.

We always aim to be clear about expectations, timelines, and deliverables before you purchase.

4. How to Request a Refund

If you believe you are eligible for a refund under Section 1 of this Policy (failure to deliver within 14 days due to an issue on our side), you must submit your request in writing within 14 calendar days of the original purchase date.

Please contact us at:

support@fastrank.io

Include the following details so we can review your request quickly:

  • Your full name and contact email
  • Order number or transaction ID
  • Website URL for the audit
  • Purchase date
  • A brief explanation of how the service was not delivered within the 14-day window as described in Section 1

We will review your request and respond in writing. Approved refunds are processed back to the original payment method. Processing times may vary depending on your bank or payment provider.

5. Questions About This Policy

If anything in this Refund Policy is unclear, we encourage you to reach out before placing an order so we can clarify how our audits and consulting services work.
