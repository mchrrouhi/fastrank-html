2. Information We Collect

We collect and process the following types of information:

a) Information you provide directly

  • Name, email address, and contact details
  • Company name, website URL, and related information you submit when requesting an audit
  • Billing details necessary to process payments (handled via our payment processors)
  • Any information you choose to provide in messages, forms, or support requests

b) Information collected automatically

When you visit fastrank.io, we may automatically collect:

  • IP address and approximate location
  • Browser type, device type, operating system
  • Pages viewed, time spent, and navigation paths
  • Referring URLs and campaign sources

c) Payment information

Payments are processed securely by third-party providers (such as Stripe). We do not store full credit card numbers on our servers. We may receive limited information about the payment status for accounting and fraud-prevention purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our Services
  • Analyse your website and deliver performance audit reports
  • Communicate with you about orders, audits, updates, and support
  • Improve our website, content, and user experience
  • Detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, or security issues
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service
  • Send you occasional service-related or marketing communications (where permitted by law and subject to your rights and preferences)

4. Legal Bases (for EEA/UK Visitors)

Where applicable data-protection law applies, we rely on one or more of the following legal bases:

  • Performance of a contract

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to remember your preferences, understand how visitors use our website, support analytics, and, where applicable, marketing attribution. You can manage cookies through your browser settings.

6. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with service providers and processors, professional advisors, authorities and regulators when required, and business transferees in connection with a merger or acquisition.

7. International Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries with different data-protection laws. Where required, we implement appropriate safeguards.

8. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to provide the Services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. When no longer needed, we delete or anonymise it.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to our processing of your data, withdraw consent, or request data portability. To exercise these rights, contact support@fastrank.io.
