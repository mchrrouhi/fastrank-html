Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)

Effective date: November 21, 2025

This Acceptable Use Policy (“AUP”) defines acceptable and prohibited uses of Fastrank services operated by Cadunlock LLC.

By accessing our website, purchasing audits, using our optimization services, or communicating with our team, you agree to comply with this AUP.

1. Acceptable Use of Our Services

Our services may only be used for lawful purposes and in a manner that:

  • Respects intellectual property rights
  • Does not violate data protection laws (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)
  • Does not harm or degrade website performance or security
  • Supports normal business operations and audit processes

2. Prohibited Activities

You may NOT use Fastrank services to:

  • Engage in illegal, fraudulent, or deceptive behavior
  • Attempt unauthorized access to servers, systems, or accounts
  • Perform malicious security testing without permission
  • Upload or share malware, exploits, or harmful code
  • Conduct DDoS attacks, traffic flooding, or load manipulation
  • Bypass or misuse Stripe payment systems
  • Scrape, clone, or extract code/content without authorization
  • Use our reports to mislead clients, investors, or third parties
  • Submit false or fraudulent refund claims

3. System Abuse & Resource Misuse

Prohibited actions include:

  • Excessive API calls (if applicable)
  • High-volume automated scraping
  • Repeated abusive requests to support or engineering
  • Actions that interfere with Fastrank infrastructure

4. Security Responsibilities

Users must maintain strong security practices, including:

  • Using accurate billing & identity information
  • Keeping access credentials private
  • Immediately reporting suspected vulnerabilities or breaches

5. Privacy & Data Protection Compliance

You may not use our services to violate privacy rights or applicable laws such as:

  • GDPR
  • CCPA
  • ePrivacy regulations
  • Intellectual property and copyright laws

Our Privacy Policy and Data Processing Agreement (DPA) outline how personal data must be handled.

6. Violations & Enforcement

Violation of this AUP may result in:

  • Temporary suspension of service
  • Permanent account or access termination
  • Cancellation of active projects
  • Refusal of future service
  • Legal action or reporting to authorities

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this AUP as needed to reflect changes in our services or regulatory requirements. The latest version will always be available on this page.
