Data Processing Addendum (DPA)

Effective date: November 21, 2025

This Data Processing Addendum (“DPA”) forms part of the agreement between Cadunlock LLC (“Fastrank”) and the customer (“Controller”) regarding the processing of personal data under GDPR and applicable privacy laws.

1. Roles of the Parties

Fastrank acts as a Data Processor and the customer acts as a Data Controller.

The Controller determines the purpose and means of processing; Fastrank processes data only on written instructions.

2. Scope of Processing

Fastrank may process the following categories of data:

  • Store URLs & performance metrics
  • Contact details (name, email, business info)
  • Technical logs & analytics
  • Support communication

Processing is limited to providing performance audits, optimization work, reporting, and support services.

3. Security Measures

Fastrank implements industry-standard technical and organizational measures including:

  • Encryption in transit and at rest
  • Access control and authentication
  • Regular security audits
  • Data minimization practices

4. Subprocessors

Fastrank may use third-party subprocessors such as:

  • Stripe (payment processing)
  • DigitalOcean / cloud hosting providers
  • Crisp (customer support)
  • Analytics & performance tools

All subprocessors are bound by GDPR-compliant terms.

5. Data Subject Rights

Fastrank assists the Controller in fulfilling GDPR obligations, including:

  • Right of access
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability

6. Data Breach Notifications

In the event of a data breach, Fastrank will notify the Controller without undue delay and provide all necessary information to assess impact and compliance steps.

7. International Data Transfers

Data may be transferred to the United States under the appropriate legal mechanisms, including Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs).

8. Return or Deletion of Data

Upon termination, Fastrank will delete or return all personal data unless retention is required by law.

9. Contact Information

For GDPR or data processing questions, contact:
