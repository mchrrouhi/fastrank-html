Support Policy

How Fastrank provides support before, during, and after optimization.

This Support Policy explains how Fastrank provides support before, during, and after Shopify speed optimization, Web Vitals engineering, and audit services. It sets clear expectations for communication, response times, support windows, and limitations.

By using our services, you agree to the support conditions described below.

1. Purpose of Support

Our support exists to:

Ensure stable deployment of optimization changes

Assist with questions related to completed work

Provide timely help for issues arising directly from our implementation

Maintain a professional and predictable support experience

Support is not a substitute for custom development or ongoing maintenance unless explicitly included in the package.

2. Support Channels

Fastrank provides support through:

Primary channels

Email support (official support email provided after purchase)

Ticket/Task system (if applicable for active sprint projects)

Not supported

No WhatsApp or personal messaging

No phone calls unless scheduled

No 24/7 real-time support

All communication must go through approved support channels for security and traceability.

3. Support Hours

Support is available during business hours:

Monday–Friday

09:00–18:00 (GMT+1)

Weekend responses may be slower.

Urgent issues (site down, broken layout) are prioritized, but not guaranteed outside business hours unless covered by a specific package.

4. Response Time Targets

We aim to respond:

Within 1 business day for normal requests

Within 4 hours for critical store-breaking issues reported during business hours

These are targets, not guaranteed SLAs, unless the client purchased a package that explicitly includes accelerated SLAs.

5. Support Windows by Package

Fastrank Launch

Support window: 14 days

Covers:

Bug fixes caused by our changes

Clarifications on the delivered work

Minor adjustments only if directly related to the optimization

Does NOT cover:

New feature requests

Issues caused by new apps installed after delivery

Changes made by external developers

Fastrank Core Web Vitals

Support window: 30 days + 1 tuning session

Covers:

Any regressions caused by our changes

Web Vitals-related fine-tuning

Clarification of technical recommendations

One follow-up adjustment session (within the 30-day window)

Does NOT cover:

Ongoing optimization beyond defined templates

Regression caused by apps, theme edits, or code changes made by the client

CRO/UX modifications unless included

Fastrank Plus

Support window: 60 days, up to 3 iterative rounds

Covers:

Regression fixes

Template/Web Vitals re-optimization

Collaboration with internal teams

Integration adjustments for global stores (if within scope)

Does NOT cover:

Unlimited redesign

Issues caused by app changes or infrastructure shifts outside our control

Custom development unrelated to optimization

6. What Is Covered in Support

Support includes:

Fixes for bugs caused directly by our optimization

Clarification questions about deliverables

Guidance on how to preserve performance after delivery

Assistance in verifying PageSpeed/Lighthouse/Web Vitals metrics

Help with theme-switching safety if change is expected

7. What Is NOT Covered

Support does not include:

UI redesign, UX changes, or theme restyling

Fixes for issues caused by:

New apps added after our work

Theme edits made by other developers

Client modifications that break optimization

CRO, A/B testing, or marketing strategy

Custom liquid development

Migration to new themes or CMS

Unlimited or long-term maintenance

If additional work is required, we will propose a new service scope.

8. Client Responsibilities

To ensure effective support, the client must:

Provide accurate access credentials

Inform us before making major theme changes

Avoid installing new apps during the support window unless coordinated

Report issues promptly with screenshots or reproduction steps

Allow reasonable time for diagnosis and testing

Failure to follow these responsibilities may void guarantees.

9. Resolution Process

When a support request is submitted:

We confirm receipt

We diagnose the issue

If caused by our work, we fix it within the support window

If not caused by our work, we provide guidance or quote a separate project

We test on staging before applying fixes to live (when applicable)

10. Escalation

If a critical issue cannot be resolved immediately:

A temporary rollback may be applied

Further work will continue on a staging theme

For Plus projects, escalation includes engineer-level review

11. End of Support Window

When the support window ends:

No further adjustments or re-optimization are included

Future changes require a new booking or maintenance plan

Guarantees no longer apply if the client alters core performance structures

12. Policy Updates

Fastrank may update this Support Policy as needed. Continued use of services implies acceptance of updated terms.
