Why merchants book a speed optimization

Most Shopify stores we audit have:

  • Slow first load on mobile (3–6s+ before anything really shows).
  • Core Web Vitals warnings in PageSpeed Insights or Search Console.
  • App bloat and heavy themes — every new feature makes the store slower.
  • Paid traffic (Meta / Google Ads) sending users to slow landing pages.

What you can expect after the sprint

We don’t promise fake 100/100 scores – we ship realistic, measurable improvements:

  • Improved LCP, CLS and INP across your key templates.
  • Leaner theme and app setup – less bloat, faster first paint.
  • Dev-ready list of changes you can reuse on other stores.
  • Clear before/after report you can show to your team or clients.
