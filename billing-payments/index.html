Billing & Payments Policy

Effective date: November 21, 2025

This Billing & Payments Policy explains how payments, fees, charges, and invoicing work when purchasing services from Fastrank, operated by Cadunlock LLC.

1. Pricing & Service Fees

All service prices displayed on our website, checkout pages, or custom proposals are expressed in EUR (€) or USD ($), depending on your region.

Pricing may differ for:

  • Website Performance Audits
  • Optimization & implementation packages
  • Custom consulting or development retainers
  • Enterprise or high-volume plans

2. Payment Processing

All online payments are securely processed by Stripe, Inc. or other approved payment providers.

  • We do not store full credit card details.
  • Stripe tokenizes and encrypts payment information.
  • Charges are processed immediately at checkout unless stated otherwise.

3. Invoicing

For every successful payment, an invoice or receipt is automatically emailed to you.

Invoices include:

  • Service purchased
  • Amount paid
  • Billing name & address
  • Tax information (when applicable)

4. Taxes, VAT & Sales Tax

Taxes may be added depending on your billing country or region.

  • EU customers may be charged VAT unless a valid VAT ID is provided.
  • US customers may be charged sales tax depending on their state.
  • International customers follow their jurisdiction requirements.

5. Failed or Declined Payments

If a payment is declined, the order will not be processed. You may retry using another payment method or contact support.

6. Refunds & Money-Back Guarantees

Refund eligibility is described in our Guarantee & Refunds Policy .

Some services may be non-refundable once work has begun or deliverables have been released.

7. Billing Disputes

If you believe a billing error has occurred, contact us within 7 days of the charge.

Unfounded chargebacks may result in service suspension.

8. Changes to Billing Terms

We may update billing practices or pricing from time to time. Changes do not affect completed purchases.

9. Contact Us

For billing or payment questions, contact:

Email: support@fastrank.io
