Billing & Payments Policy

How Fastrank handles payments, taxes, invoices, and billing procedures.

This Billing & Payments Policy explains how Fastrank processes payments, handles taxes, manages billing information, and applies charges for speed optimization, audits, and related services.

By purchasing any Fastrank service, you agree to the billing terms outlined below.

1. Payment Methods

Fastrank accepts payments through:

Stripe (recommended)

Major credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, AMEX)

Apple Pay / Google Pay (when available)

We do not accept bank transfers, crypto, PayPal, or offline payments unless explicitly agreed in writing.

2. Pricing Structure

Fastrank provides services as:

One-time service fees (Launch, Core Web Vitals, Plus)

Optional add-ons or extended work blocks

No subscriptions unless explicitly offered

All prices displayed on the website are in USD, unless stated otherwise.

Local currency equivalents (CAD/AUD) shown on the site are approximate and processed at the current exchange rate through Stripe.

3. When Charges Occur

Payment is collected:

Immediately at checkout for standard packages

Before any work begins for custom or Plus-level projects

After client approval for additional scope items (If the client requests extra work not included in the purchased package)

Work does not begin until payment is successfully processed.

4. Taxes & Fees

Depending on your country or region:

Stripe may automatically apply sales tax, VAT, GST, or regional digital service taxes

Currency conversion fees may apply depending on your bank

Fastrank does not manually add taxes outside Stripe’s automated tax system.

All taxes will appear clearly at checkout before payment.

5. Billing Information

You must provide accurate billing information, including:

Name

Business or store name

Email

Billing address

Country and region

Incorrect or incomplete billing information may delay delivery of services.

6. Invoices & Receipts

After successful payment:

A payment receipt is automatically emailed

A full invoice including tax details is generated via Stripe

Invoices can be resent upon request via support

Fastrank does not generate manual invoices unless required for enterprise/Plus contracts.

7. Failed or Declined Payments

If a payment fails or is declined:

The order is not confirmed

No work will begin

You will be prompted to update payment details

Stripe may retry the charge automatically depending on your bank

If payment remains unpaid, the service request is cancelled.

8. Refund Handling

Refunds are governed by the Guarantee & Refunds Policy, which defines:

When refunds apply

The conditions required

What happens if performance guarantees are not met

Refunds (when approved) are always processed back to the original payment method only.

Processing times vary:

Stripe: usually 5–10 business days

Banks: may take longer depending on region

9. Additional Work & Scope Changes

Any request outside the purchased package—such as:

Additional templates

Fixing issues caused by new apps

Post-support-window requests

Custom development or redesign

Extended Web Vitals tuning

—will require a separate quote and payment.

If the client changes theme, installs a new app, or modifies code during an active project, additional fees may apply if rework is needed.

10. Chargebacks & Disputes

Clients agree to:

Contact Fastrank first before opening a dispute

Give us a fair opportunity to resolve the issue

Understand that chargebacks without valid cause are considered breach of terms

If a chargeback is initiated:

Support is immediately paused

Work is stopped

Access is revoked

Legal and administrative fees may be pursued

We resolve most issues quickly without needing disputes.

11. Currency, FX Rates & International Payments

When paying from outside the US:

Stripe automatically converts payments to USD

Exchange rates depend on your bank/provider

Small variations may appear on statements due to FX fluctuations

Fastrank is not responsible for bank-side FX fees or international transfer fees.

12. Subscription/Recurring Billing (if introduced later)

If Fastrank introduces maintenance or monthly optimization plans:

Clients will be notified in advance

All recurring charges will require explicit opt-in

Cancellation terms will be clearly listed

Currently, no services auto-renew.

13. Billing Contact

All billing questions should be sent to the official Fastrank support email.

We will respond with invoice copies, billing clarifications, and payment-status updates.

14. Policy Updates

Fastrank may update this Billing & Payments Policy at any time. Continued use of services means you accept the updated terms.
