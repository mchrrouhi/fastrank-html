GDPR Compliance & Data Rights

Effective date: November 21, 2025

This page explains how Fastrank, operated by Cadunlock LLC, complies with the EU & UK General Data Protection Regulation (“GDPR”) and how individuals can exercise their data rights.

1. Data Controller

131 Continental Dr, Suite 305

2. Legal Bases for Processing (Article 6 GDPR)

We process personal data under one or more of the following bases:

  • Performance of a contract (providing audits or consulting)
  • Legitimate interests (analytics, improvements, security)
  • Legal obligations (tax, accounting)
  • Consent (cookies, marketing if applicable)

3. Your Data Rights Under GDPR

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access — Request a copy of your personal data.
  • Right to rectification — Fix inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to erasure (“right to be forgotten”).
  • Right to restrict processing.
  • Right to object to processing (including marketing).
  • Right to data portability.
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time.
  • Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

4. How to Submit a GDPR Request

To exercise any GDPR rights, send an email to support@fastrank.io with the subject line: “GDPR Request”.

We may request additional verification to confirm your identity before providing or deleting any data.

5. Data Retention Policy

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to provide our services, fulfill legal obligations, or resolve disputes. When data is no longer needed, it is securely deleted or anonymized.

6. International Transfers (GDPR Chapter V)

Your data may be processed in the United States or other countries. Where required, we use:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
  • Data Processing Agreements (DPAs)
  • Industry-standard security protections

7. Automated Decision-Making & Profiling

Fastrank does not use automated decision-making systems that produce legal or significant effects on individuals under Article 22 GDPR.

We may update this page periodically to reflect regulatory changes or internal policy updates. Continued use of our services indicates acceptance of the latest version.
