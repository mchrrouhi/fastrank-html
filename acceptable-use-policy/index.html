Acceptable Use Policy

Guidelines for safe, compliant, and professional use of Fastrank services.

This Acceptable Use Policy defines the rules, responsibilities, and acceptable behavior for clients using Fastrank’s Shopify performance, speed optimization, Web Vitals engineering, audits, and related services.

By engaging with Fastrank (“we”, “our”, “the Service”), the client (“you”, “your business”) agrees to respect all conditions below.

1. Purpose of This Policy

This AUP exists to:

Protect the integrity, security, and performance of client stores.

Ensure safe handling of store access, data, and technical environments.

Maintain a professional, respectful, and legally compliant relationship.

Prevent misuse of Fastrank services or abuse of our team.

2. Prohibited Store Types

We do not provide services to stores involved in any of the following:

Illegal or regulated items without proper authorization

Counterfeit goods

Weapons, explosives, or tactical gear

Drugs, controlled substances, or related paraphernalia

Illegal gambling or betting

High-risk content

Adult content or explicit pornography

Hate, violence, extremist content

Malware, spyware, or security-compromised systems

Websites distributing pirated software, cracked tools, or stolen assets

Unethical commercial practices

Misleading claims or fake reviews

Dropshipping stores selling prohibited/unsafe items

Stores operating under false identity or stolen brand assets

Fastrank reserves the right to decline or terminate service if a store is deemed high-risk or legally problematic.

3. Client Conduct & Behavior

Clients must agree to:

Treat our team respectfully and professionally.

Avoid abusive, aggressive, or harassing communication.

Provide accurate business information and honest representation of their store.

Avoid requesting illegal or unethical modifications.

Not bypass or circumvent payment, support, or operational systems.

We reserve the right to stop work immediately if communication becomes abusive or unsafe for the team.

4. Store Access Rules

To ensure safe optimization:

You must grant the correct Shopify collaborator or staff permissions.

You may not share compromised, unsafe, or stolen credentials.

You must not modify theme files during active optimization unless coordinated with our team.

Any unauthorized change during the project may void guarantees.

5. Technical Limitations & Safety

You agree not to misuse our work in ways that could:

Break the site intentionally

Introduce malicious code

Add unstable apps/scripts that conflict with optimization

Deploy unsafe liquid/custom code without informing us

If your team or another developer overwrites or undoes our work, guarantees no longer apply.

6. Misuse of Reports, Audits, or Deliverables

Our reports, audit documents, and technical recommendations:

Are for your internal use only

Cannot be resold, republished, or used as your own service offering

Cannot be given to third-party developers as “your” deliverables without credit

Violation may lead to service termination.

7. Service Abuse

Prohibited behaviors:

Filing fraudulent refund requests

Threatening chargebacks to force free work

Repeatedly requesting work outside the purchased package

Expecting unlimited support beyond defined windows

Abuse leads to service cancellation with no refunds.

8. Legal Compliance

You agree not to use Fastrank services in any activity that violates:

Shopify’s Terms of Service

Local and international laws

GDPR, CCPA, or relevant privacy regulations

Intellectual property and copyright laws

9. Termination Rights

Fastrank may terminate service immediately (with refund only when appropriate) if:

Your store violates this AUP

You provide false information

Communication becomes abusive

You refuse to follow technical safety guidelines

Illegal activity is detected

10. Changes to This Policy

Fastrank may update this AUP as necessary. Continued use of our service implies acceptance of any updated terms.
