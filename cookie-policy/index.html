Cookie Policy

Effective date: November 21, 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how Fastrank by Cadunlock LLC (“we”, “us”, “our”) uses cookies and similar technologies on our website fastrank.io. It also describes your choices regarding cookie preferences and how to manage consent.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help improve your browsing experience, enable essential website features, and allow us to analyze website performance.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: Required for site functionality and security.
  • Preference Cookies: Store user settings and improve usability.
  • Statistics Cookies: Provide anonymous insights to improve performance.
  • Marketing Cookies: Help personalize ads and track conversions.

3. Third-Party Cookies

Fastrank uses third-party tools that may place cookies, including:

  • Google Analytics & Google Ads
  • Stripe (billing protection cookies)
  • Crisp Chat widget
  • Tracking tools used for audits and performance monitoring
