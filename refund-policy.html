Refund Policy

Last updated: November 14, 2025

At Cadunlock LLC, we offer a comprehensive website audit service designed to help you improve your website's performance, SEO, and user experience. This Refund Policy explains when you may be eligible for a refund.

1. Refund Eligibility

✅ 100% Satisfaction Guarantee

We guarantee your complete satisfaction with our audit service. If you're not entirely satisfied with the quality and thoroughness of your website audit report, we offer a full refund within 30 days of your purchase.

You are eligible for a full refund if:

  • You are not satisfied with the quality, depth, or usefulness of the audit report
  • The audit report does not meet the specifications outlined in your selected plan
  • We were unable to complete the audit due to technical issues on our end
  • You request a refund within 30 days of your purchase date

2. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact our support team within 30 days of your purchase:

Contact Information:
Email: support@fastrank.io
Include your order number and reason for the refund request

Please include the following information in your refund request:

  • Your name and email address used for the purchase
  • Order number or transaction ID
  • Website URL that was audited
  • Reason for the refund request
  • Any specific feedback about the audit report

3. Refund Processing

Once we receive your refund request:

1

We will acknowledge your request within 24 hours

2

Our team will review your case and the audit report

3

If approved, we will process the refund within 3-5 business days

4

The refund will be credited to your original payment method

4. Refund Timeframe

Depending on your payment method and financial institution:

  • Credit Cards: 3-10 business days
  • PayPal: 3-5 business days
  • Bank Transfers: 5-10 business days

5. Non-Refundable Situations

While we strive to accommodate all reasonable requests, refunds may not be available in the following cases:

  • Refund requests made more than 30 days after purchase
  • Attempts to use our service fraudulently or in violation of our Terms of Service
  • Requests where the audit was completed successfully according to specifications but the customer changed their mind about needing the service

6. Alternative Solutions

Before requesting a refund, we encourage you to reach out to our support team. We may be able to:

  • Provide additional clarification or explanation of the audit results
  • Address specific concerns about the audit methodology
  • Offer a revised or supplementary analysis if needed
  • Provide personalized guidance on implementing the recommendations

7. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Refund Policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting on our website. Your continued use of our services after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the modified policy.
