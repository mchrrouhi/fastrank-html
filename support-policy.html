Support Policy

Effective date: November 21, 2025

This Support Policy describes how Fastrank, operated by Cadunlock LLC, provides customer service, communication, and issue resolution for all purchased services.

1. Support Channels

Support is available through the following channels:

  • Email: support@fastrank.io
  • Live chat via Crisp
  • Project-specific communication (when applicable)

2. Support Availability

Our support team operates during standard business hours:

  • Monday to Friday
  • 9:00 – 18:00 (UTC+1)
  • Excluding holidays

Responses outside these hours may be delayed depending on team availability.

3. Response Times

Typical response times depend on request priority:

  • High Priority (site down / critical issues): 4–8 hours
  • Standard Requests: 12–24 hours
  • Low Priority / General Questions: 24–48 hours

4. What Support Includes

Support covers topics directly related to purchased services:

  • Performance audit clarifications
  • Bug explanations and guidance
  • Fixes included in optimization packages
  • Technical recommendations
  • Follow-up questions on delivered work

5. What Support Does Not Include

The following items are outside the scope of standard support unless covered in a paid plan:

  • Full theme customization or redesign
  • Custom app development
  • Ongoing maintenance not included in a plan
  • Third-party system errors
  • Issues caused by customer-side code modifications

6. Customer Responsibilities

To receive timely and effective support, customers must:

  • Provide accurate store access when required
  • Describe issues clearly with screenshots when possible
  • Avoid changes that may interfere with ongoing work
  • Check email regularly for updates from our team

7. Issue Escalation

Complex issues may be escalated internally to senior engineers or performance specialists. Escalation timelines vary depending on workload and investigation complexity.

8. Support Termination

Support for a completed service ends once all agreed deliverables have been reviewed and accepted, unless a support add-on or maintenance plan has been purchased.
