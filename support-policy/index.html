Support Policy

Effective date: November 21, 2025

This Support Policy explains how Fastrank by Cadunlock LLC (“Fastrank”, “we”, “our”) provides customer support for all services, including performance audits, optimization work, and ongoing technical assistance.

1. Support Channels

Fastrank offers support through the following official channels:

  • Email: support@fastrank.io
  • Live Chat: via Crisp on our website
  • In-dashboard communication (for subscribed customers)

Support requests must be submitted through one of these channels to ensure proper tracking.

2. Support Availability

Our support team operates during the following hours:

  • Monday – Friday: 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM (UTC+1)
  • Weekend: Limited coverage for urgent cases

3. Response Times

We aim to respond to support requests within the following timeframes:

  • Standard Requests: 12–24 hours
  • Urgent Issues: within 4–8 hours
  • Enterprise or Managed Plans: priority handling

Delays may occur during peak load or holidays.

4. Scope of Support

Support includes, but is not limited to:

  • Assistance with audit reports and recommendations
  • Bug identification and guidance
  • Performance troubleshooting
  • Clarification of optimization tasks
  • Post-implementation checks

Support does not include full theme rebuilds, unrelated development tasks, marketing work, or third-party app debugging unless included in a paid plan.

5. Customer Responsibilities

To ensure efficient support, customers agree to:

  • Provide accurate, complete information when submitting a request
  • Grant temporary access when needed for diagnosis
  • Implement recommended fixes unless Fastrank handles implementation

6. Emergency Issues

Critical performance failures or site downtime related to Fastrank work will receive immediate priority handling.

7. Updates to This Policy

Fastrank may update this Support Policy as needed. Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of the updated terms.

8. Contact Information

For support-related questions, contact:
