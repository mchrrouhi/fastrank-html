Support Policy

Effective date: November 21, 2025

This Support Policy outlines how Fastrank, operated by Cadunlock LLC, provides customer support for audits, implementation services, and technical consulting (“Services”). Our goal is to deliver fast, reliable, and expert support to ensure you receive maximum value from our work.

1. Support Channels

Email Support: support@fastrank.io

Live Chat Support (Crisp): Available on all pages for pre-sales and technical questions.

Client Portal (when applicable): Shared links containing audits, deliverables, or implementation instructions.

2. Support Availability

  • Monday–Friday: 9:00–18:00 (UTC+1)
  • Average response time: 4–12 hours
  • 48h Audit Delivery Time applies only to paid audits
  • Weekend responses are limited to urgent technical matters

3. Types of Support Included

  • Pre-sales questions and service clarification
  • Technical questions about your audit results
  • Help implementing recommendations from the audit
  • Issue diagnosis related to Core Web Vitals, speed, or Shopify/WooCommerce performance
  • Bug analysis caused by our own implementation work

4. What Support Does NOT Cover

  • Fixing issues caused by third-party developers or apps
  • Custom theme development not included in your package
  • Feature requests or new functionality outside the scope of purchased services
  • Ongoing maintenance (available as a separate service)
  • Server-level debugging on hosting providers

5. Severity Levels & Response Times

  • High (Site broken / checkout issues): Response within 2–6 hours.
  • Medium (Performance issue affecting sales): Response within 6–12 hours.
  • Low (General questions / clarifications): Response within 12–24 hours.

6. Implementation Support

If you purchase an Implementation Pack, support includes applying fixes directly to your store/theme and validating improvements after deployment.

7. Refunds & Guarantees

Refund conditions follow our Guarantee & Refund Policy. Support does not guarantee specific performance numbers unless explicitly stated in your package.

8. Contact Us

For any support request, contact:

Email: support@fastrank.io

Support chat available Monday–Friday, 9 AM–5 PM (New York Time, UTC-5)
